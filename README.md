

## **1. Project Title / Overview**

### **Sales and Traffic Insights of BunnyHugs Toy Store**

Developed a **dynamic, interactive Power BI dashboard** to analyze:
- Top-performing products  
- Conversion funnels  
- Website traffic trends  
- User behavior  
- Campaign performance  

---

## **2. Description**

Two dashboards were created to present insights derived from in-depth analysis of the BunnyHugs Toy Store datasets:

- **Website Traffic Dashboard** – Designed for the **Marketing Manager** to monitor user engagement, traffic channels, and conversion funnels.  
- **Sales Performance Dashboard** – Designed for the **Sales Manager** to evaluate product performance, refund patterns, and cross-selling opportunities.

---

## **3. Tech Stack Used**

- **Power BI Desktop**  
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)**  
- **Data Modeling**  
- **Conditional Formatting**  
- **Tooltips** – to provide additional context within visuals  

---

## **4. Data Source**

The analysis utilized **six CSV files**:

| File | Description |
|------|--------------|
| **orders.csv** | One row per completed order |
| **order_items.csv** | One row per product item within an order |
| **order_item_refunds.csv** | One row per refunded product item |
| **products.csv** | Product catalog containing item details |
| **website_sessions.csv** | One row per user website session |
| **website_pageviews.csv** | One row per page viewed during a session |

**Data Model:** Transactional Snowflake Schema

---

## **5. Business Context & Highlights**

**BunnyHugs Toy Store** is an e-commerce startup specializing in **stuffed animal toys**.  
As the company prepares for its next funding round, the **Analytics Team** was tasked by the **CEO** to:

- Craft a **data-driven growth story** for potential investors.  
- Build dashboards to help stakeholders monitor **key metrics** and **KPIs**.  
- Support **data-driven decision-making** for marketing and sales optimization.

---

### **Dashboard Goals**
- Provide full visibility into **website traffic** and **sales performance**.  
- Identify **top-performing products** and **cross-sell combinations**.  
- Understand **customer journeys**, **traffic sources**, and **conversion funnels**.  

---

### **Key Visuals**
- How do customers **navigate through the website** – *Decomposition Tree*  
- What are the **trends in website traffic and usage** over time – *Bar Chart*  
- Which are the **top-performing and most refunded products** – *Bar Chart*  
- Compare the sales of **different products over time** – *Line Chart*  
- What are the **most frequently cross-sold product combinations** – *Matrix*  
- **Conversion Funnel** – *Waterfall Chart*  
- **Sales Completed vs. Sales Returned Ratio** across products – *Stacked Bar Chart*  

---

### **Business Impact**

- Identified the most effective **marketing channels** and **frequent user journey paths**.  
- This enables the marketing team to **design targeted campaigns** and **optimize the website** for smoother customer experiences.

#### **Sales Optimization**
- Identified top **product combinations** to increase average order value.  
- Analyzed **refund rates** to detect quality or satisfaction issues.  
- Price analysis helps to make pricing decisions based on product demand and performance.
